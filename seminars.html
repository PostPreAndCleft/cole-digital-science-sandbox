Friday Afternoon Reese Seminar Series

From 2020 to 2025, the Friday Afternoon Reese Seminar series was a weekly seminar series hosted by Drs. Cole and Reese. In 2021, we incorporated the Marine Biological Laboratory (MBL) Neuroimaging Seminar series.

About the series

Topics include synaptic structure, imaging workflows, and computational approaches to microscopy. The series featured researchers from across structural cell biology and neuroimaging.

Format

30–60 minute research talks followed by discussion. Over 100 seminars were organized across NINDS and the Marine Biological Laboratory.
