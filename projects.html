Projects

A snapshot of active and recent initiatives in synaptic ultrastructure and tomography. Each entry includes the current phase and a public summary.

How projects ship

Each project is designed with a clear biological question, an analysis path, and a public artifact—code, methods, or datasets—when appropriate.

Transsynaptic assembly mapping

Mapping synapse-spanning assemblies and their remodeling during activity using electron tomography and molecular labeling.

Status: Active research

Open tomography analysis platform

Developing a web-based analysis platform with plug-ins for advanced segmentation and machine-learning workflows to accelerate volumetric microscopy.

Status: Tool-building

Synaptic architecture in disease risk

Identifying ultrastructural signatures linked to neurodegenerative risk by comparing synaptic organization across conditions.

Status: Study design
