About the lab-style practice

Structural cell biology · electron tomography · synaptic architecture

My research uncovers how nanoscale synaptic structures govern organization, signaling, and plasticity. I focus on electron tomography, light microscopy, and computational analysis to reveal the physical architectures that sustain learning and memory.

Research lens

My program maps transsynaptic assemblies, identifies their molecular components, and characterizes how assemblies remodel with activity. In parallel, I develop open, scalable analysis tools to accelerate tomographic discovery.

Core themes

Synaptic ultrastructure Transsynaptic assemblies Electron tomography Open-source analysis

These themes guide my research, tool-building, and collaborative projects. Each project includes a public summary and research artifacts when possible.
